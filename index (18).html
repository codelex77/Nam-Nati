OUR STORY

ALL OF US,
TOGETHER.

Nam'Nati is a name rooted in love. Owner and visionary Unami Makali named it after her mother's affectionate nickname for her — "Unati," a Xhosa name meaning "Unami." It evolved into Nam'Nati, meaning "all of us together."

Nam'Nati is a proudly local subsidiary of Kitchen Poetry, nestled in the foyer of the Letshego Building at Zambezi Towers, right in the Gaborone CBD.

More than a restaurant, it's a declaration — a space that listens, adapts, and brings people together over food that honours both local heritage and international flair.

"We are open to critiquing so that we give our clients an outstanding culinary experience." — Unami Makali
